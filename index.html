Hello I'm Otis,

I'm from the PNW I love taking photos and getting outside. Anyone who has lived here knows the struggle of taking photos in the rain so for the few sunny days we get year-round I enjoy being able to capture that. This site is a culmination of my photography and photography related projects. The top section represents my more recent as well as favorite works.
